Temple Inn & Suites - Services

Wondering why you should stay with Temple Inn & Suites? Here are some of the services and amenities that we offer!

Some services and amenities vary by hotel location.

Free Amenities

During your stay, you will have access to the following free amenities:

  • Continental Breakfast
  • Exercise / Workout / Yoga Room
  • Indoor Heated Pool
  • High-Speed Wifi Internet and Smart TVs in Every Room
  • Valet Parking
  • Shuttle to and from the Temple
  • Family History Consultation and Ordinance Card Print Service

Specialized Services

Temple Patrons will also have access to the following specialized services:

  • Changing Rooms Access and Bathroom Facilities for Temple Patrons
  • Low-cost Babysitting and Playroom for Patron's Children
  • Long-term Stay Accommadtions for Temple Missionaries (see Full Time Temple Missionaries link below for details.)
  • Temple History Themed Reception and Gathering Area
  • Wedding Reception Hall (See Receptions link below for details.)
  • Family History Search Center for assisting in Family History Temple Work
  • Low-cost Temple Clothing Cleaning and Ironing Service
