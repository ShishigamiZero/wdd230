Temple Inn & Suites - Receptions

Looking for a Temple themed reception venue? Look no further!

Reception Venue

The reception venue at each location is designed to preserve the aesthetic of the local temple:

  • Designs have been carefully selected based on the design of the temples they serve
  • Venues can accommadate between 300 and 400 people, pending location
  • Reception Halls include dance floors, and additional room can be made as needed
  • Catering for Receptions are available (see Catering and Services), special preperation needs available upon request
  • Venue preparation services are available upon request (See Catering and Services)
  • Gathering Area for Temple Guests are located next to the Reception Venue
  • If Catering is declined, a seperate food preperation area is available at no-cost to the guest

Catering and Other Services

We can help make your wedding and reception easier with our professional catering and other reception services. Here are just a few of the options:

  • Professional Catering with five options for meal types including: Hor d'oeuvres, simple meals, full course meals, wedding cakes and other dessert options
  • Professional DJ with appropriate fun and romantic music options to fit the temple theme
  • Venue set-up by our trained staff, or setup by your local ward if requested
  • Changing rooms for the wedding party if they wish to change prior to the reception
  • Professional clean up services by our trained staff, or cleanup by your local ward if requested
  • Plans and options vary by service, please speak with the location's reception planner for details
