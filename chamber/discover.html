Discover the Treasure Valley!

Located within 50 miles of the Treasure Valley are many of Idaho's mountain ranges. There you can find adventure, or a quick weekend getaway where you can go boating, camp, hunt, fish, and enjoy all the pleasures the outdoors have to offer!

Treasure Valley is home to a large Farmer's Market that takes place every weekend from March to October. Come see the products and produce our local residents have to offer!

Treasure Valley boasts one of the most active and athletic communities in the country. If you're looking to get fit, Treasure Valley is a great place to start working on a new you!

Freak Alley is one of the biggest art attractions in the Treasure Valley. This local, open space gallery gives artists large spaces to express their artistic nature. Come see the new and cool ways these local artists express themselves!

Treasure Valley is a center of learning! From the many local libraries to the largest University in Idaho, Treasure Valley believes in education, and there are many places to learn something new here!

There are no shortages of local eateries, pubs, bars, and even food trucks! Treasure Valley has some of the most restaurants per capita in the country, where you can try something new everyday.

Treasure Valley has a great concert and entertainment scene. There is something to do every night of the week with concerts, movies, plays and even opera to see!

Business is big in the Treasure Valley. It is the largest hub for business in the state of Idaho, and it's continually growing!

Besides the mountains, there are many local recreational facilities such as parks, hiking trails, swimming pools, and other places to go enjoy the outdoors in the area. Come and explore the Treasure Valley and see what it has to offer!

Treasure Valley Statistics and Facts!

Did you know that Treasure Valley is the largest city in Idaho? Some facts about the place we call home may surprise you! Here are some interesting facts that you might not have been aware of:

  1.   Around 40% of Idaho's population calls Treasure Valley Home. This means that out of the 1,900,923 people in Idaho, 760,370 people live in Treasure Valley!
  2.   Six of the top ten best High Schools in the Idaho are in Treasure Valley. It is also home to higher education, with four colleges or universities in the area.
  3.   Weather is mostly sunny, with some wintery snow days, and summer highs between 90 and 100 degrees.
  4.   Treasure Valley is located in Idaho's high desert region.
  5.   In Treasure Valley, there is more than agriculture. Many world renown businesses come from here, including Albertsons and Hewlett Packard.
  6.   It's the 8th fastest growing metropolitan area in the United States.
  7.   There are lots of recreational activities to do in and around Treasure Valley. The Greenbelt offers a long trail for biking or walking, and there are many parks and entertainment venues in the area.
  8.   Treasure Valley is close to Idaho's forests and mountains, making for even more things to go do! Camping, fishing and hunting are favorite past times of our residents!
